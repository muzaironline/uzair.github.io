Stop These Habits!! That Make You Age Faster

by adminJune 6, 2020June 8, 2020
Spread the love

Feeling older than you? In addition to genetics, there are many factors that contribute to the age you want.

Bright Side shares 10 habits you should leave to look young without Botox or cosmetic surgery. Remember that it is never too late to start, so change this lifestyle now.

1. Low-fat diets

© pixabay  

To prevent wrinkles, you should take omega-3 fatty acids, which will help keep your skin soft and supple. A low-fat diet can cause the loss of this substance, leading to premature aging of the skin.
Here are other foods that will allow you to stay young.

2. Sipping through a straw

© pixabay  

When drinking drinks with straw, you should rinse your lips, which can cause stripes and wrinkles in your mouth. The same applies to smokers. Try to drink directly from the glass and do your best to quit if you are smoking. It is a long and arduous process, but the benefits of quitting smoking are huge.
Here are other tips on how to maintain healthy skin.

3. Sleeping on your side

© pixabay  

Sleeping on your side by squeezing your pillow face can cause wrinkles on the cheeks and chin. Experts say that lying on your back is the best place to sleep, stay young, stay fresh, and relax.
If you need more tips on how to sleep well, we invite you to read this article.

4. Bad posture

© unsplash  

Bad posture distorts the spine of its normal alignment, resulting in abnormal tension in the muscles and bones. These losses cause pain and fatigue and often lead to permanent disability.
We recommend these exercises to improve your appearance.

5. Using sun protection only on vacation

© freejpg  

Excessive exposure to UV rays is the main reason for premature skin aging. To protect yourself from the harsh effects of the sun, it is important to wear your sunscreen even in cloudy weather, as clouds can block up to 20% of UV rays.
If you want to tan without burning, consider these tips to secure the best suntan.

6. Overusing your heating system

© pixabay  

Excessive use of artificial heating can make your indoor air very dry. Your skin and hair will become dry, resulting in more wrinkles. Try to reduce the heat in your home.
We recommend reading this article for more tips on how to maintain healthy skin.

7. Saying no to sugar

© pixabay  

We all know that sugar is harmful to us, but there is also stress, and sometimes you have to give yourself a treat and surrender to your sweet tooth.

Here is an article with quick and delicious chocolate dessert recipes for your guilt.

8. Watching too much TV

© unsplash  

A study has found that every hour you spend watching TV shortens your life span by 22 minutes. Experts recommend getting up every half hour, even if you are in bed and wandering around the table.
If you are working out and have trouble getting up all the time, we recommend these exercises that you can do in the workplace.

9. Lack of sleep

© unsplash  

Adults need at least 7 hours of sleep each night. Not getting enough sleep will not only make you fatigued but also shorten your life. If you have energy deficiency, mental retardation, or weight loss, experts recommend sleeping quickly.
This animated video shows you how insomnia can affect your brain.

10. Stress due to lack of organization

© tumblr  

Numerous studies have shown that chronic stress can cause serious damage to cells, which can accelerate the aging process. Don’t be afraid, leave what you can do tomorrow.
We recommend some activities to help reduce stress.
